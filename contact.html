The ICT in Health Informatics course provides an in-depth understanding of the pivotal role Information and Communication Technology (ICT) plays in transforming healthcare systems. It covers the application of technology in managing health information, facilitating clinical decision-making, improving patient outcomes, and ensuring seamless communication between healthcare providers. Students explore foundational concepts such as electronic health records (EHRs), health information exchanges (HIEs), and telemedicine, alongside emerging technologies like mobile health (mHealth), artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, and big data analytics. The course emphasizes system interoperability, data privacy, and security, highlighting how these elements contribute to building efficient and patient-centered healthcare systems.
